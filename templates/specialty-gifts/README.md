# Specialty Gifts Template

A modern, elegant template designed for gift shops and specialty retail businesses.

## Perfect For:
- Gift shops
- Boutique gift stores
- Personalized gift services
- Gift hampers and baskets
- Corporate gifting services
- Specialty items retailers

## Features:
- Hero section with featured products
- Browse by occasion (Birthday, Anniversary, Wedding, Corporate, etc.)
- Browse by price range
- Featured products carousel
- Product categories grid
- Services highlight section
- About us section
- Contact form with inquiry options
- Footer with social media links

## Color Scheme:
- Uses warm, inviting colors suitable for gift shops
- Elegant typography
- Mobile-responsive design

## Components:
- Header with navigation
- Hero banner
- Category browser
- Product showcase
- Services section
- Contact form
- Footer

## Customization:
All content is easily customizable including:
- Business name and logo
- Product categories
- Featured items
- Colors and branding
- Contact information
